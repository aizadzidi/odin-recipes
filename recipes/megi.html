Megi Recipe

Home

This is one of the most delightful megi recipe you may find!

It has become a local main dish in Malaysia since centuries.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of instant megi (any flavor)
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Vegetables of your choice (e.g., carrots, cabbage, green onions)
  • Protein of your choice (e.g., chicken, tofu, shrimp)
  • Chili sauce or soy sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot.
  2. Add the instant megi noodles to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions (usually 3-5 minutes).
  3. If using, crack an egg into the pot and stir gently to create egg ribbons.
  4. Add your choice of vegetables and protein to the pot. Cook until the vegetables are tender and the protein is cooked through.
  5. Turn off the heat and add the seasoning packet that comes with the instant megi. Stir well to combine all ingredients.
  6. If desired, add chili sauce or soy sauce for extra flavor.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious megi!
