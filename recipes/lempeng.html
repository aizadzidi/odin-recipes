Lempeng Recipe

This is one of the most delightful lempeng recipe you may find!

It has become a local main dish in Malaysia since centuries.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. Gradually add the coconut milk and water to the dry ingredients, whisking until you achieve a smooth batter.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  4. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan, spreading it out to form a thin, round pancake.
  5. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on one side until bubbles form on the surface and the edges start to lift.
  6. Flip the lempeng and cook for another 1-2 minutes on the other side until golden brown.
  7. Remove from the pan and repeat with the remaining batter, greasing the pan as needed.
  8. Serve the lempeng warm with your favorite sides such as kaya (coconut jam), butter, or curry.
