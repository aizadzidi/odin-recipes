Sardin Recipe

This is one of the most delightful sardin recipe you may find!

It has become a local main dish in Malaysia since centuries.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sardin in tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 chili, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic, saut√© until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the sliced chili and chopped tomato, cook for a few minutes until the tomato softens.
  4. Pour in the can of sardin along with its tomato sauce.
  5. Stir gently to combine all ingredients.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Let it simmer for about 5-7 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your meal!
